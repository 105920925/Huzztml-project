G01- Software Developer

Reference: G01SD Reports To: Technical Lead Salary Range: $85,000 - $115,000

Position Overview

We are seeking a talented Software Developer to join our growing development team. You will be responsible for developing and maintaining software applications, collaborating with cross-functional teams, and implementing innovative solutions to complex problems.

Key Responsibilities

  1. Design, develop, and maintain efficient, reusable, and reliable code
  2. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define, design, and ship new features
  3. Work with front-end and back-end technologies as needed for project goals
  4. Implement automated testing to ensure code quality and reliability
  5. Participate in code reviews and contribute to documentation
  6. Optimize applications for maximum speed and scalability

Qualifications & Skills

Essential:
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field
  • 3+ years of experience in software development
  • Proficiency in at least one modern programming language (Java, Python, C#)
  • Experience with relational databases and SQL
  • Knowledge of software design patterns and principles
  • Understanding of data structures and algorithms
  • Experience with version control systems (Git)
Preferable:
  • Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP)
  • Knowledge of containerization (Docker, Kubernetes)
  • Understanding of CI/CD pipelines
  • Experience with microservices architecture
  • Knowledge of test-driven development
  • Experience with Agile development methodologies

Cybersecurity Analyst

Reference: CA512 Reports To: Security Operations Manager Salary Range: $90,000 - $120,000

Position Overview

We are looking for a skilled Cybersecurity Analyst to protect our systems and data from cyber threats. In this role, you will be responsible for implementing security measures, monitoring for breaches, and responding to security incidents to ensure our digital assets remain secure.

Key Responsibilities

  1. Monitor security systems for potential threats and security breaches
  2. Conduct vulnerability assessments and penetration testing
  3. Develop and implement security policies and procedures
  4. Investigate security incidents and provide response
  5. Configure and maintain security tools and technologies
  6. Provide security awareness training to employees
  7. Stay updated on the latest cybersecurity trends and threats

Qualifications & Skills

Essential:
  • Bachelor's degree in Cybersecurity, IT, or related field
  • 2+ years of experience in cybersecurity or related IT role
  • Knowledge of security frameworks (NIST, ISO 27001)
  • Experience with security tools (SIEM, IDS/IPS, firewalls)
  • Understanding of network security concepts
  • Knowledge of vulnerability management
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
Preferable:
  • Cybersecurity certifications (CISSP, CEH, Security+)
  • Experience with cloud security (AWS, Azure, GCP)
  • Knowledge of scripting languages (Python, PowerShell)
  • Experience with incident response
  • Familiarity with compliance regulations (GDPR, HIPAA)
  • Experience with threat intelligence platforms

Application Process

Our hiring process is designed to find the best talent while providing you with a great candidate experience:

  1. Application Review: Our hiring team reviews all applications
  2. Initial Screening: Phone call to discuss your experience and expectations
  3. Technical Assessment: A task related to the position you're applying for
  4. Team Interviews: Meet with potential team members and managers
  5. Final Decision: We make an offer to the successful candidate

We aim to complete the entire process within 2-3 weeks to respect your time.
